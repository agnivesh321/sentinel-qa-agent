# Domain Roulette Strategy

## Selected Domain

`hidden.reviews`

## Why This Domain Wins

The domain has a direct product meaning. It suggests a tool that uncovers reviews that are not visible in ordinary review dashboards.

## Sponsor Criteria Mapping

### Creative Interpretation

The product treats "hidden reviews" literally: reviews and complaints hidden across public shadow channels and private company notes.

### Technical Execution

The repo includes:

- Deterministic Python agent engine
- Evidence extraction and source weighting
- Cluster ranking
- Action-plan generation
- Static browser dashboard
- Local API
- Tests
- JSON, CSV, Markdown, and HTML exports

### Product Polish

The first screen is the working command center. It includes input, hidden signal score, source mix, pattern ranking, action plan, evidence board, and domain proof.

### Strength Of Concept

Customer-intelligence workflows are a real SaaS category. Founders and product teams already pay for review monitoring, social listening, NPS, product analytics, and support tooling. Hidden Reviews AI connects those fragmented signals into founder actions.

### Domain Connection

The product name, UX, data model, and value proposition all reinforce the selected domain.

## Winning Angle

Most Domain Roulette submissions will make a funny or light product from a domain. This one makes a believable SaaS business from a memorable domain.

## Honest Probability

If submitted with just the repo: 15-20%.

With hosted dashboard, strong video, clean Devpost story, and gallery image: 35-50%.

If final polish includes live deployment and crisp 3-minute demo: 45-60%.
