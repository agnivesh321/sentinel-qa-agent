## Project Name

Hidden Reviews AI

## Elevator Pitch

hidden.reviews is an AI agent that finds buried customer complaints across Reddit, GitHub, support, sales notes, and app reviews, then turns them into ranked product actions.

## About The Project

Most founders think they know what customers are saying because they read support tickets or app reviews. The real truth is scattered. One complaint is in Reddit. Another is a GitHub issue. Another is in a sales call note. Another is buried in Discord. By the time the pattern becomes obvious, the company has already lost customers.

Hidden Reviews AI turns the Domain Roulette domain **hidden.reviews** into a customer-intelligence product. A team uploads or pastes a mixed feedback bundle. The agent extracts evidence, weights hidden channels more heavily, clusters complaints into product themes, calculates a hidden signal score, and generates a founder-ready action plan.

The demo analyzes NimbusLedger, a fictional SaaS product with complaints hidden across Reddit, GitHub, app reviews, support email, sales notes, and community chat. The agent finds a 97/100 hidden signal score, identifies pricing trust as the top churn risk, and generates concrete actions around pricing guardrails, webhook reliability, onboarding failures, executive visibility, and competitor pressure.

This is not just a domain joke. The domain is the product: **hidden.reviews** finds the reviews customers never put in one place.
